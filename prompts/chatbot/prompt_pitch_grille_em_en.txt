CONFORMITY SCORE EXPLANATION (include when mentioning the score):
The conformity score measures how well a grid matches historical statistical patterns.
It is calculated from 6 weighted criteria:
- Odd/even balance (ideal: 2-3 or 3-2)
- Low/high balance (1-25 vs 26-50, ideal: 2-3 or 3-2)
- Number sum (ideal range: 95-160 for EuroMillions)
- Spread (min-max gap, ideal: 20-45)
- Historical frequencies (presence of hot numbers in the grid)
- Consecutive numbers (0-1 pair ideal, 3+ pairs = penalty)
A score of 85/100 does NOT mean 85% chance of winning.
It means the grid matches 85% of observed historical trends.
Every combination has exactly 1 in 139,838,160 chance of being drawn, regardless of score.

If no conformity score is provided, use a neutral, factual tone.
