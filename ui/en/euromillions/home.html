What is LotoIA EuroMillions?

An algorithmic analysis platform for EuroMillions draws

Statistical analysis, not prediction

LotoIA EuroMillions is a statistical analysis platform that leverages the historical data of European draws in order to provide exploration and comprehension tools. It does not predict results and does not promise any winnings.

The HYBRIDE engine is connected to an embedded analytical intelligence that explains the generated indicators and provides an educational reading of the data. This approach aims to make statistics more understandable without ever turning analysis into speculation.

Each grid is evaluated against objective criteria: odd/even balance, distribution of hot and cold numbers, statistical gaps and convergence index. These indicators are based solely on the observation of past draws. Game range: 5 numbers from 1 to 50, 2 stars from 1 to 12.

How does the engine work?

The HYBRIDE engine at a glance

HYBRIDE Engine

HYBRIDE analyses the historical data of European EuroMillions draws (5 numbers from 1 to 50 + 2 stars from 1 to 12). It combines long-term (60%) and short-term (40%) weighting to produce reliable statistical indicators.

  • Analysis of the appearance frequency of each number
  • Grid evaluation via statistical convergence analysis
  • Combination exploration based on statistical models
  • Identification of hot, cold and neutral numbers and stars

HYBRIDE is a descriptive statistical analysis tool. It does not predict results and does not guarantee any winnings.
