10 January 2026 Technical

HYBRIDE EuroMillions Engine ‚Äî Architecture & Exploration Principles

The HYBRIDE EuroMillions engine is the algorithmic core of the European module. It explores draw statistics by combining two complementary approaches, adapted to the 5+2 format:

  • Historical analysis (60%): study of number and star distribution across all past draws.
  • Recent trend analysis (40%): focus on the last 24 months to capture ongoing dynamics.
  • Separate analysis: the 5 balls (1-50) and the 2 stars (1-12) are treated as two distinct combinatorial spaces.
  • Balance filters: verification of dispersion, parity and number distribution within each grid.

LotoIA EuroMillions is an analysis and exploration tool, not a prediction tool. The distribution of results remains subject to the laws of pure chance.
